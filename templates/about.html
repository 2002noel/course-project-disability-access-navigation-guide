Disability Access Navigation Guide

Through the website, we will provide the accessibility information of classrooms across the campus. We want to prodive the good resource for people who are mobility impaired, and visually impaired, then they have better experience with the campus and knowing all the benefit they have.

Team Information

We're a team of computer science students at Western Washington University located in Bellingham Washington who strive to create software tools for accessible computing.

We are divided into two teams, one of which will be in charge of the campus map and the visual data, and another one will in charge of the report-updated information, website creation, and database management.
