About the Disability Access Navigation Guide

Through this website, we will provide accessibility information regarding classrooms across campus. We want to provide excellent and thorough resources for people who are mobility impaired and visually impaired, so that they have an equitable experience while navigating campus.

Team Information

We are a team of computer science students at Western Washington University (WWU) located in Bellingham Washington that strive to create accessible software tools

We are divided into two teams, one of which will be in charge of the campus map and the visual data, and the other team will take charge of the report-updated information, website creation, and database management.
