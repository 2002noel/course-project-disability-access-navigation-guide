Building Accessibility Features

  • Elevator
  • Button activated entrances are located on the North and South sides of the building.
  • The West elevator accesses to all floors including split levels.
  • The East elevator only accesses to whole floor levels.
  • Access to Arntzen Hall is via the ground and 3rd floor skybridge.
  • Accessible restrooms are located on the Concourse / Ground (not 1st) floor at the west end of the building.
  • This building has Accessible, Gender Neutral Restrooms.
