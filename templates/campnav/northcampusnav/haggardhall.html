Disability Access Navigation Guide

Haggard Hall

Building Accessibility Features

  • Elevator
  • Button activated entrances are located on the Northwest, Northeast, and Southeast sides of the building.
  • The Northeast entrance is located under the Haggard Hall/Wilson Library sky-bridge.
  • The 2nd floor sky-bridge provides wheelchair access to the Wilson Library.
  • Elevators provide access to all floors.
  • The elevator near High Street only provides access to classrooms on all floors, not the library.
  • The central elevator only provides access between floors 2 and 3 of the library.
  • This building has Accessible, Gender Neutral Restrooms.
